Hajji Alejandro was a famous singer during the 70s and 80s.
He is known for his Filipino adaptation of The 5th Dimension's song
"Worst That Could Happen", his adaptation being titled "Panakit-Butas".
He is also popular for his remix of the song "Nakapagtataka", originally
written by Jim Paredes of the APO Hiking Society. Aside from remixes and
adaptations, he has his fair share of original songs like
"Tag-Araw, Tag-Ulan", "May Minamahal", and many more.
One such song, "Kay Ganda ng Ating Musika", won first place in the first ever
Metro Manila Popular Music Festival (or Metropop for short).
